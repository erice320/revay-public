01 — Design Direction

Dark Enterprise — Already Established

The Rev.io Summit 2026 site (already built in Claude Design) is the visual north star. The design system exists — this is a rollout exercise.

🎨 Color System

Deep navy to near-black backgrounds. White/off-white primary text. Cyan-teal for all CTAs and highlights. Subtle atmospheric depth in hero sections.

✍️ Typography

Bold modern sans for headlines with italic serif contrast on emphasis words — "Prepare for Tomorrow." treatment. All-caps muted labels for overlines.

🃏 Cards & Surfaces

Dark glass panels with very subtle borders (rgba white 8–12%). No white boxes on white backgrounds. Soft shadow depth. Card borders glow lightly on hover.

⚡ CTAs & Buttons

Pill-shaped. Cyan fill for primary (matching "Register Now →"). Ghost/outline for secondary. Consistent across every page — no more multiple button styles.

02 — What's Broken Now

5 Visual Problems to Fix

These are the specific issues Claude Design needs to solve on every page.

1

Split Personality — Dark Hero, White Below

Pages start with a dark header then drop into bright white below the fold. Every product page, every industry page. The dark needs to sustain through the entire page — no white sections below the hero.

2

White Card Tiles on White Background

The PSA feature grid and similar sections use bright white cards on a white/light gray background. Zero depth, no visual hierarchy. Replace with dark glass panels that have actual presence.

3

Stats Are Buried Instead of Celebrated

"2x revenue." "300% higher ops efficiency." "5:1 ROI." These are the most powerful proof points on the site. Currently displayed as small icon+text blocks. Should be large, bold visual anchors — the way the summit hero handles "400+ Expected Attendees."

4

CTA Button Inconsistency

Multiple button styles across pages — different colors, shapes, weights, hover states. Unify to: cyan pill (primary) + ghost pill (secondary). One system everywhere.

5

Integration Logos Look Like a Vendor List

Currently displayed as a cluttered multi-color grid. Should be white-on-dark with category grouping (Accounting, RMM, CRM, Carriers). Each category gets a dark glass card, logos go white/muted inside.
